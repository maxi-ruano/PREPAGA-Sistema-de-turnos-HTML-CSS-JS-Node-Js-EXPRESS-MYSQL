The Beginnings


We were born in 1989 with the construction of the Suizo Argentina Clinic and Maternity Hospital, the group's first investment and the initial link for successive undertakings that positioned us as one of the leading organizations in this activity. This was followed by the creation of our own prepaid medicine in 1994: Swiss Medical Medicina Privada, which has since become one of the most important health service companies in Argentina, providing coverage to more than 950,000 associates. Later, the Group entered the insurance market through the acquisition of 100% of St. Paul Argentina, a company that stands out in the national and international market for its exceptional solvency level. This is how Swiss Medical Insurance was born. We are one of the main groups in Argentina dedicated to the protection of people, made up of 8 clinics, 12 outpatient medical centers, ECCO Emergency and Prevention, SMG Cells, SMG Seguros, SMG LIFE, the Salta Institute, SMG ART, the Swiss Medical Foundation and Blue Cross & Blue Shield in Uruguay.
